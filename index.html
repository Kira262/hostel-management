Hostel Management System

About this Website

A survey rendered that the current ways of managing a hostel are a mess. They're time consuming, require paper based applications and highly inefficient. A solution needs to be in place for a problem which is faced by thousands of students across the country. In an age of digitization, it is a folly that hostels are still using outdated paper based systems to manage the day to day functioning of the hostels. There needs to be change in the current ways to make it easier for both the student and the administration to perform the daily interactions.

Query

The students can use the admin provided password to login to their account. If they are facing any problem with the hostel facilities provided in their room, they can generate a query for that problem through their account.

Absence Request

The leave request will now be sent to the admin dashboard where it can be approved by the admin. The information about the leave submitted by the student is also sent to the student's parents via mail.

Attendance

The students that are outside the hostel are visible on the admin dashboard. A mail is automatically sent out to the students parents informing them about the violation of the deadline, 30 minutes after the violation.
